About me

Ben Kartchner has always been fascinated with computers and how they work. For as long as he can remember he has been using them to play games and mess around and do cool stuff. However, despite this infatuation with technology, Ben was confused for a long time on what he wanted to major in. He initally thought of studying Mechnical Engineering because he liked math and physics, but while on his mission he connected with his inner child and decided to pursue an Animation degree from BYU, with a minor in CS. After taking the intro to programming class, Ben connected further with his inner child and realized this is what he needed to do. Ben is currently trying to get into the CS Animation program at BYU, but if he doesn't get in he will continue on studying in the CS field. He is especially excited for this web programming class and all of the cool skills he will acquire while being in it.
